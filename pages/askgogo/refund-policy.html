AskGogo Returns and Refunds Policy

  1. Introduction: Welcome to AskGogo. We provide a peer-to-peer trading platform and e-commerce services. This policy covers our approach to returns and refunds.
  2. Scope: This policy applies to all products and services sold through AskGogo, including digital goods and physical products.
  3. Returns for Physical Goods:
    • Eligibility: Returns of defective or not as advertised physical goods are accepted within 30 days of purchase. Customers must provide photo evidence of the defect.
    • Process: Upon approval, the item must be returned in its original packaging. Please email support@askgogo.co.za to initiate a return.
  4. Returns for Digital Goods and Services:
    • Eligibility: For digital goods or services, customers must provide a valid rationale for dissatisfaction. We will facilitate communication with the service provider.
    • Process: If the reason aligns with the provider's service delivery standards, a refund will be considered. Contact support@askgogo.co.za for assistance.
  5. Refunds:
    • Condition: Valid returns will be refunded to the original payment method.
    • Method: Refunds will be processed within a specified number of days after return acceptance.
  6. Customer Responsibilities: Customers are responsible for understanding the terms before purchase and providing necessary documentation for returns.
  7. Contact Information: For queries or clarifications, contact us at support@askgogo.co.za.
  8. Policy Updates: AskGogo reserves the right to modify this policy and advises customers to review it periodically.
